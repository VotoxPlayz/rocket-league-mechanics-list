Rules & Requirements

These rules ensure fairness and consistency for all submissions on the RLML.

Record Submission Rules

  • The video must clearly show the successful completion of the mechanic.
  • The video must be recorded in an unmodded, vanilla Rocket League environment. BakkesMod is allowed, but any mods that alter physics or provide an unfair advantage are not.
  • The submission can be from either a ranked match or free play.
  • The video link must be public and accessible (e.g., YouTube, Streamable, Gfycat).

Mechanic Submission Rules

  • The mechanic must be a unique, difficult, and visually appealing shot or sequence.
  • The video must clearly demonstrate how the mechanic is performed.
  • An accurate and detailed description of the mechanic is required to be considered for the list.
  • The submission must adhere to the same mod and video rules as record submissions.
  • Inappropriate names or content will be rejected.
